TigerTalks

About TigerTalks

TigerTalks is an event that will be held prior to the main hackathon that aims to build confidence in students who have never attended one before or are beginners. There will be various talks on topics that provide students with skills to help them build their first hack.

Food and Prizes

We will be holding a raffle where you can have a chance to win a prize! In addition to the raffle, free pizza from Pizza Tree will also be provided at TigerTalks.

Date and Location

TigerTalks will take place on April 6th, 2019 in Lafferre Hall. Room c1205 will host beginner talks and Room c1206 will host advanced talks. For times of specific talks, see below.
